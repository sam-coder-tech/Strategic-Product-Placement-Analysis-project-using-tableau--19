# 📊 Strategic Product Placement Analysis: Unveiling Sales Impact with Tableau Visualization

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the impact of **product positioning on sales performance and consumer behavior in a retail environment**.

The goal of this project is to understand how different **product placement strategies influence customer purchasing patterns and overall sales performance.**

Using **Tableau for data visualization**, the project explores relationships between:

- Product categories  
- Pricing strategies  
- Consumer demographics  
- Store foot traffic  
- Promotional activities  

The analysis helps identify which **product positioning strategies are most effective in increasing sales and improving product visibility.**

The insights from this project can help businesses **optimize their product placement strategies, marketing decisions, and revenue growth.**

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

A retail company wants to understand **how product positioning affects sales and consumer behavior.**

They collected data related to:

- Product placement  
- Pricing strategies  
- Promotional campaigns  
- Customer demographics  

The objective is to **analyze this data using Tableau visualization** to identify patterns and generate **actionable insights** that can help improve **sales performance and marketing strategies.**

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset used in this project is a **CSV file containing retail sales information** such as:

- Product Category  
- Product Position  
- Price  
- Competitor Price  
- Sales Volume  
- Consumer Demographics  
- Store Foot Traffic  
- Seasonal Sales Data  
- Promotional Information  

This dataset helps analyze how **product placement, pricing strategies, and consumer behavior influence sales performance.**

---

## 📈 Visualizations Created

The following visualizations were created in **Tableau**:

- **Average Sales Volume vs Product Category**
- **Competitor Price vs Product Price**
- **Average Sales Volume by Product Category by Product Position**
- **Consumer Demographics vs Sales Volume**
- **Product Category vs Price**
- **Average Sales Volume by Product Category by Season**
- **Foot Traffic vs Average Sales Volume**
- **Promotion Impact on Product Category, Price, and Sales Volume**

These visualizations help identify patterns in **sales trends, consumer preferences, and product positioning effectiveness.**
